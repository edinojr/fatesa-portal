# Prompts para Arte Sacra Medieval (Estilo Manuscrito Iluminado)

Estes prompts foram desenvolvidos para a geração de imagens de arte conceitual sacra medieval para as lições.

## 1. Retratos (Portraits)
**Prompt:**
An illuminated manuscript style portrait of [NOME OU DESCRIÇÃO DA PESSOA, ex: Saint Paul writing a letter / King David playing the harp], medieval sacred art, golden halo around the head, vibrant tempera paint on aged parchment texture. Rich colors including lapis lazuli blue and vermilion red, heavy gold leaf accents on the borders. Flat perspective, Byzantine icon style, highly detailed religious illustration.

## 2. Mapas e Paisagens (Maps & Landscapes)
**Prompt:**
An ancient medieval map and geographical landscape painting of [NOME DA REGIÃO OU LUGAR, ex: The Sinai Peninsula with Mount Sinai / The ancient city of Jerusalem with its walls]. Gothic manuscript illumination style, stylized mountains and rivers, cartography elements from the middle ages. Aged vellum texture, decorated margins with gold leaf filigree, rich earthy tones, lapis lazuli water, symbolic sacred geography illustration.

## 3. Cenas Históricas (Historical Scenes)
**Prompt:**
A grand historical scene depicting [DESCRIÇÃO DO EVENTO HISTÓRICO, ex: The Council of Nicaea with bishops gathered / Ancient Roman soldiers in a historical backdrop]. Medieval fresco and tapestry style art, flat narrative perspective, rich storytelling layout. Deep pigment colors, gold leaf halo highlights on key figures, aged parchment background, Romanesque and Gothic architectural details, highly symbolic sacred art.
